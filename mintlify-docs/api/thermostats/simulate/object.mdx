## Endpoints

[**`/thermostats/simulate/hvac_mode_adjusted`**](./hvac_mode_adjusted)

Simulates having adjusted the [HVAC mode](/capability-guides/thermostats/understanding-thermostat-concepts/hvac-mode) for a [thermostat](/capability-guides/thermostats). Only applicable for [sandbox devices](/core-concepts/workspaces#sandbox-workspaces). See also [Testing Your Thermostat App with Simulate Endpoints](/capability-guides/thermostats/testing-your-thermostat-app-with-simulate-endpoints).

[**`/thermostats/simulate/temperature_reached`**](./temperature_reached)

Simulates a [thermostat](/capability-guides/thermostats) reaching a specified temperature. Only applicable for [sandbox devices](/core-concepts/workspaces#sandbox-workspaces). See also [Testing Your Thermostat App with Simulate Endpoints](/capability-guides/thermostats/testing-your-thermostat-app-with-simulate-endpoints).
