---

This guide walks you through connecting your ecobee thermostats to Seam. Your thermostats must already be set up and working in the ecobee app before proceeding.

## 1. Create an ecobee account

If you don't already have one, download the [ecobee app](https://www.ecobee.com/en-us/installation/) and create an account. Add your ecobee thermostats in the app and confirm they are online.

## 2. Connect ecobee to Seam

Use the login credentials for your ecobee account to log in through the [Seam Connect Webview](/core-concepts/connect-webviews) and authorize Seam to access your devices.

<Info>
  Make sure that you're authenticating with the user to whom the devices are
  registered. ecobee devices can only be registered to a single user.
</Info>

## 3. Verify the connection

After completing the Connect Webview, confirm that your ecobee thermostats appear in your Seam workspace and show an **online** status.

---

## Troubleshooting

If your devices don't appear after connecting:

- Confirm that your ecobee thermostats are online in the ecobee app.
- Verify that you logged in with the same ecobee account that owns the thermostats.
- Wait a few minutes for the initial sync to complete, then refresh your device list.
