## Overview

This guide walks you through connecting your ASSA ABLOY Vostio access control system to Seam. Your Vostio system must already be configured and operational before proceeding.

---

## Step 1: Create Your Vostio API Credentials

1. Log in to the Vostio dashboard.
2. In the left sidebar, click **Settings**, then select **Integrations**.
3. On the **Guest API** tab, click **Create credentials**.
4. Save the generated credentials — if you lose them, you cannot retrieve them. You would need to create new credentials and delete the old ones.

---

## Step 2: Connect to Seam via Connect Webview

1. Open the [Connect Webview](/core-concepts/connect-webviews) provided by your application.
2. Select **Vingcard Vostio** as the provider.
3. Enter your Vostio Guest API credentials.
4. Once authorized, your Vostio system, entrances, and encoders appear in Seam.

---

## Step 3: Verify the Connection

After connecting, verify that your Vostio ACS system shows as connected, your entrances (guest doors, common doors, entrance doors, elevators) appear, and your encoders are listed.

---

## Troubleshooting

### Authorization fails in Connect Webview

Verify that you are using the correct Vostio Guest API credentials. If you have lost your credentials, log in to the Vostio dashboard and create new ones under **Settings > Integrations > Guest API**.

### Entrances not appearing after authorization

Ensure your Vostio system has doors and rooms configured. Only entrances that are active in the Vostio system appear in Seam.

### Encoder not found

Confirm that your card encoders are registered and active in the Vostio system. Inactive encoders do not appear in Seam.
