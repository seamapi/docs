---
title: 'Tedee Setup Guide'
description: 'Connect your Tedee locks to Seam.'
---

To use Seam with Tedee locks, ask the device owner to complete the steps below. Locks must already be installed and connected to a Tedee smart bridge before proceeding.

### 1. Set up the Tedee mobile app

1. Download and install the [Tedee mobile app](https://tedee.com/knowledge-base/tedee-app/) and create an account if you don't have one.
2. In the Tedee app, add your Tedee devices.

<Info>
  You must add your Tedee devices through the Tedee mobile app. You cannot add
  devices through the Tedee web portal.
</Info>

### 2. Connect Tedee to Seam

1. Note your login credentials for the Tedee app.
2. In the form to connect Tedee to Seam, enter those credentials and click **Submit** to complete.

---

### Troubleshooting

If devices don't appear after connecting your account:

- Verify that each lock is online and connected to a Tedee smart bridge in the Tedee app.
- Confirm you added your devices through the Tedee mobile app, not the web portal.
