title: 'Honeywell Resideo Setup Guide'
description: 'Connect your Honeywell Resideo thermostats to Seam.'
---

This guide walks you through connecting your Honeywell Resideo thermostats to Seam. Your thermostats must already be set up and working in the Resideo app before proceeding.

## 1. Create a Resideo account

If you don't already have one, download the [Resideo app](https://www.resideo.com/us/en/resideo-smart-home-app/) and create an account. Add your Honeywell Resideo thermostats in the app and confirm they are online.

## 2. Connect Honeywell Resideo to Seam

Use the login credentials for your Resideo account to log in through the [Seam Connect Webview](/core-concepts/connect-webviews) and authorize Seam to access your devices.

## 3. Verify the connection

After completing the Connect Webview, confirm that your Honeywell Resideo thermostats appear in your Seam workspace and show an **online** status.

---

## Troubleshooting

If your devices don't appear after connecting:

- Confirm that your Honeywell Resideo thermostats are online in the Resideo app.
- Verify that you logged in with the same Resideo account that owns the thermostats.
- Wait a few minutes for the initial sync to complete, then refresh your device list.
