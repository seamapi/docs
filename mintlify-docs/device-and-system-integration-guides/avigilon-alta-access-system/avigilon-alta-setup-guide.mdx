## Overview

This guide walks you through connecting your Avigilon Alta access control system to Seam. Your Avigilon Alta system must already be set up and working before proceeding.

---

## Step 1: Gather Your Credentials

Obtain the credentials for a user within the Avigilon Alta organization to which you want to connect.

---

## Step 2: Verify User Permissions

1. In a web browser, navigate to and log in to your [Avigilon Alta dashboard](https://control.openpath.com/login).
2. In the settings for the user, verify the user's permissions as follows:
   - Make sure that **Portal access** is selected (enabled).
   - Make sure that the **Roles** for the user include **Super Admin**.

   <img src="/images/avigilon-alta-user-permissions.png" alt="Verify user permissions in the Avigilon Alta dashboard." style={{width: "550px", height: "auto"}} />

---

## Step 3: Connect to Seam

Use the credentials for the user to log in to the [Connect Webview](/core-concepts/connect-webviews) to add your Avigilon Alta access system and devices to Seam.

---

## Step 4: Verify the Connection

After connecting, verify that your Avigilon Alta system and devices appear in your Seam workspace and show a **connected** status.

---

## Troubleshooting

### Connection fails during login

- Double-check that the user credentials are correct.
- Confirm that the user has **Portal access** enabled and the **Super Admin** role assigned.

### Devices not appearing after connecting

- Verify that your devices are registered and online in the [Avigilon Alta dashboard](https://control.openpath.com/login).
