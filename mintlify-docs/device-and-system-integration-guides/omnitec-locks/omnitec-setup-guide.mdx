## Overview

This guide walks you through connecting Omnitec hotel locks to Seam. The integration requires an [Omnitec Rent&Pass Gateway](https://www.omnitecsystems.com/products/rent-and-pass/gateway-online), which provides the Wi-Fi connectivity that enables real-time communication between Seam and your Omnitec locks.

<Warning>
  Seam only supports Omnitec locks that are online through a gateway. Offline-only Omnitec locks (without a gateway connection) are not supported.
</Warning>

---

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, ensure you have:

- An [Omnitec Rent&Pass Gateway](https://www.omnitecsystems.com/products/rent-and-pass/gateway-online) installed and connected to Wi-Fi
- Omnitec locks paired with the gateway and visible in the Rent&Pass app

---

## Step 1: Locate Your Integrator Credentials

The credentials used for the Seam integration are **not** the same as the login email and password used to access the Rent&Pass app.

To find your integrator credentials:

1. Open the Omnitec Rent&Pass app.
2. Navigate to the **Integrator Credentials** section.
3. Note the **Username** and **Password** displayed there.

<Info>
  If the "Integrator Credentials" section is not visible in your Rent&Pass app, contact Omnitec support to have it enabled on your account.
</Info>

---

## Step 2: Connect to Seam via Connect Webview

1. Open the [Connect Webview](/core-concepts/connect-webviews) provided by your application.
2. Select Omnitec as the provider.
3. Enter the **Integrator Credentials** from the Rent&Pass app (not your regular app login).
4. Once authorized, your online Omnitec locks appear as devices in Seam.

---

## Step 3: Verify the Connection

After connecting, verify that your locks appear in the Seam Console under Devices. Each lock should show as online and report lock/unlock and access code capabilities.

---

## Troubleshooting

### Authorization fails in Connect Webview

Make sure you are using the **Integrator Credentials** from the Rent&Pass app, not your regular app login email and password. These are different credentials. If the "Integrator Credentials" section is not visible in the app, contact Omnitec support.

### Locks not appearing after authorization

Ensure the Rent&Pass Gateway is powered on and connected to Wi-Fi. Only locks that are online through the gateway are visible to Seam.

### Lock shows as offline

Check that the lock is within range of the gateway and that the gateway's Wi-Fi connection is active. Re-pair the lock with the gateway if necessary.

### Gateway connection issues

Verify that the gateway's Wi-Fi network has internet access. The gateway needs to reach Omnitec's cloud servers to relay commands from Seam.
