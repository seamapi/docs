## Overview

Tado is a European smart home company focused on energy-efficient climate control for residential properties. Their smart thermostats connect to existing heating and cooling systems and provide remote control, scheduling, and energy-saving features like geofencing and open window detection. Tado is popular across Europe and offers both heating and cooling control for a wide range of HVAC systems.

Seam integrates with Tado thermostats to give you programmatic control over heating, cooling, and climate settings. You can configure HVAC modes, create climate presets, schedule thermostat settings, and monitor temperature thresholds through the Seam API.

## Device Provider Key

To create a [Connect Webview](/core-concepts/connect-webviews) that enables your users to connect their Tado devices to Seam, include the `tado` device provider key in the `accepted_providers` list. For more information, see [Customize the Brands to Display in Your Connect Webviews](/core-concepts/connect-webviews/customizing-connect-webviews#customize-the-brands-to-display-in-your-connect-webviews).
