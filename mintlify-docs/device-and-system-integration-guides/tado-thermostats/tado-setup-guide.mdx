## Overview

This guide walks you through connecting your Tado thermostats to Seam. Your Tado devices must already be set up and visible in the Tado app before proceeding.

---

## Step 1: Connect to Seam via Connect Webview

1. Open the [Connect Webview](/core-concepts/connect-webviews) provided by your application.
2. Select **Tado** as the provider.
3. Log in with your Tado account credentials.
4. Once authorized, your Tado thermostats appear as devices in Seam.

---

## Step 2: Verify the Connection

After connecting, verify that your Tado thermostats appear as devices and show as online with heating and cooling capabilities.

---

## Troubleshooting

### Authorization fails in Connect Webview

Verify that you are using the correct Tado account credentials.

### Devices not appearing after authorization

Ensure your Tado thermostats are set up and online in the Tado app. Only devices that are active and connected appear in Seam.
