This guide walks you through connecting your igloohome locks to Seam. Your locks must already be set up and working in the igloohome app before proceeding.

## 1. Create an igloohome account

If you don't already have one, download the igloohome mobile app and create an account. For more information, see "Download App & Sign in" in the [igloohome app quick start guide](https://support.igloohome.co/support/solutions/articles/35000183120-igloohome-app-quick-start-guide).

## 2. Add your igloohome devices

In the igloohome mobile app, add your igloohome locks. For more information, see "Pair your lock" in the [igloohome app quick start guide](https://support.igloohome.co/support/solutions/articles/35000183120-igloohome-app-quick-start-guide).

## 3. Set up the igloohome Bridge (optional)

To configure your igloohome smart locks remotely, trigger lock and unlock actions, and use custom access codes, install the [igloohome Wi-Fi Bridge](https://www.igloohome.co/en-US/products/bridge) and pair your locks to the bridge.

<Info>
  The igloohome Bridge is not required for offline access code (algoPIN)
  provisioning. It is only needed for remote lock/unlock and custom access
  codes.
</Info>

## 4. Connect igloohome to Seam

Use the login credentials for your igloohome account to log in through the [Seam Connect Webview](/core-concepts/connect-webviews) and authorize Seam to access your devices.

## 5. Verify the connection

After completing the Connect Webview, confirm that your igloohome locks appear in your Seam workspace and show an **online** status.

---

## Troubleshooting

If your devices don't appear after connecting:

- Confirm that your igloohome locks are paired in the igloohome mobile app.
- Verify that you logged in with the same igloohome account that owns the locks.
- Wait a few minutes for the initial sync to complete, then refresh your device list.

If your devices show as offline:

- Verify that the igloohome Bridge is powered on and connected to Wi-Fi.
- Check that each lock is within range of the bridge and paired to it.
- Note that locks without a bridge will only support offline access codes (algoPIN).
